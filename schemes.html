Some More Government Schemes Identified for Ideation & Startups

Sr. No. Scheme Eligibility
1 DSIR PRISM (Department of Scientific and Industrial Research)

1. Any Indian Citizen with an innovative idea or invention from any educational background.

2. The applicant has to be a single person applying under Phase 1 and Phase 2 category.

3. The projects relating pure software development and those involving pure academic research are not eligibl.
2 TIDE 2.0 EIR (Technology Incubation and Development of Entrepreneurs Entrepreneur-in-Residence )

1.Applicant should be an Indian citizen.

2. Graduate in Sciences/ Engineering/ Biotechnology/ Medicine etc.

3. Individual/team should have an idea/prototype

4. The solution should be ICT enabled serving a pressing problem across any of the above mentioned domains.

5.At least one member from the team should be willing to pursue the idea full time.

3 IIT-K IAIN FELLOWSHIP (India Agritech Incubation Network (IAIN))

1. The candidate must be a student/ fresh graduate pursuing engineering or any course within Science and Engineering disciplines.

2. Must be an Indian national below the age of 35.

3. The candidate should be developing an Agritech solution for the small-holder farmers in Uttar Pradesh.
